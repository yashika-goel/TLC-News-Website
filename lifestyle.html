1. Neither any type nor any amount of alcohol is safe, can cause cancer: WHO
As per WHO, there's no safe type or amount of alcohol that doesn't affect health. Alcohol can cause seven types of cancer, including bowel and female breast cancer, WHO stated. "Ethanol causes cancer...as the compound breaks down in the body, which means...any beverage containing alcohol, regardless of its price and quality, poses a risk of developing cancer," WHO's research stated.
